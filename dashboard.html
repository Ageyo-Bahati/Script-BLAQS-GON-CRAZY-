BLAQS GON CRAZY

Genre: Action, Drama, Romance, Comedy

Location: Kisumu City


*Synopsis:*

In the streets of Kisumu where everyone’s got hustle, *JAYMO*- a broke, foul mouthed lawyer opens an underground court to settle petty beefs and street drama. But this ain’t your ordinary courtroom- people bring their mess: Cheating partners, unpaid debts, stolen hustles- and is packed with wild insults and dirty secrets. Jaymo’s trying to keep shit together and get paid, but things get complicated when he falls for *NYAMBURA*, a sharp- tongued hustler with chaotic past. Their toxic romance is as explosive as the cases they handle, arguments one second, lip-locked the next.


Between messy love triangles, jealous exes and shaddy hustlers , Jaymos' "court" becomes the hottest show in town, attracting drama so real it gets dangerous. In Kisumu, it’s ride or die and everyone’s talking shit on the way.

Main Characters:

*Jaymo*: A sarcastic lawyer, always scheming to make a buck but barely keeping his life in order.

Nyambura: A beautiful but volatile hustler- she's smart, savage and knows how to push every button.

Pato: Jaymos goofy best friend, quick with jokes but slower than molasses when it comes to life choices.

Baba Reggie: A corrupt businessman with old beefs and new enemies lurking around every corner.

Shiro: A flirty waitress who’s everybody’s problem but no one’s girlfriend.

Opening Scene – “First case:The Dirty Hustle.”

(Location: Jaymo’s background court inside a smoky bar. plastic chairs scattered and cheap beer bottles everywhere. The room is noisy until Jaymo walks in and slams his hands on the table.)

Jaymo:"Yo! everyone, shut the fuck up. Y’all here to talk shit or settle this beef?"

(The crowd falls quiet as young couple, Denno and Becky, step forward.)

Deno:(Yelling) " She stole my side hustle, Jaymo! I gave this girl my sauce recipe, and now she's out here sellin' it without me!

Becky:(Rolling her eyes) " Nigga please! you gave me shit. I made it better-now you cryin ' like a lil' bitch 'cause you broke."

Jaymo:(Grinning) " Yo, Becky... I gotta say, that does sound like a solid business plan."

Denno: "Fuck that! I want my cut!"

Jaymo: (chuckling)"Nigga, you couldn’t even cut it in school - now you want a cut here? Get the fuck outta here."

( The crowd laughs, Becky smirks,and Jaymo claps his hands to move on.)

Jaymo: " Case dismissed. You both toxic as hell - y'all deserve each other. Next!"

Scene – “Romantic Scene-Jaymo and Nyambura’s First Kiss”

(Location: A late night street food joint by the lake. Jaymo and Nyambura sit side by side, munching on the same smokies. The chemistry is thick, even though they keep roasting each other.)

Nyambura: (teasing) " You always this broke, or you just trying to impress me?"

Jaymo: (laughs)" Girl, I’m too broke to even fake bein' rich. But you knew that already so what's good?"

Nyambura: (Softly) "What's good is... you ain’t scared to run your mouth over me."

( They lock eyes, the banter turning into something else, Jaymo leans in and Nyambura doesn’t pull away. The kiss is slow,heated, and reckless - like two people who know they’ll be fighting again in five minutes.)

Comedy Scene – “Pato Gets in a fight”

(Location: Outside a matatu stage. Pato tries to jump a line and an angry vendor catches him.)

Vendor: "Aye! where the fuck you think you goin', big man?)

Pato: "Relax, bro. I just wanted to see the front seats- you'll actin' like I stole the whole bus."

Vendor : "Man, get the fuck back in line before I rearrange your face!"

( Pato throws his hands up, trying to de-escalate.)

Pato: " Yo, mbro chill! you fightin' over a seat? That's broke ass behavior, fam."

Vendor:( Steps closer)" say that again, nigga. I dare you."

( Before Pato can respond, the vendor swings - but misses, slipping on a banana peel. The whole crowd bursts out laughing, and Pato grins like a fool.)

Pato: " God don’t like ugly, bro. Now move, before you embarrass yourself more."

" Climactic Scene - Jaymo and Nyambura Confront Baba Reggie."

( Location: A rooftop near lake Victoria. Jaymo and Nyambura stand face to face with Baba Reggie whose been double-crossing them for weeks.)

Baba Reggie:(smirking) Y’all came here to cry over some petyy shit? You gotta grow the fuck up."

Jaymo: " Nah, old man. We came to collect - so pay up or get fucked up."

(Baba Reggie pulls a gun, but Nyambura moves faster- she snatches it out of his hands and points it right back at him.)

Nyambura:(grinning) " What now, huh? You ain’t so tough without your little toy, are you?"

Baba Reggie: " Y’all niggas don’t know who you messin' with."

Jaymo:( Laughs ) "Nah, you don't know who you're fuckin' with."

( Nyambura and Jaymo exchange a look, then toss the gun off the roof into the lake.)

Jaymo: That's how we handle shit in Kisumu, old man. No guns - just mouths and hands."


---

Ending Scene - The Court Is Lit

( Location: Back at the bar-courtroom, business is booming. Nyambura and Jaymo run the place together now, arguing with each other between cases.)

Jaymo:( Yelling at a client)" Listen, if you don’t stop talkin' bullshit, I'mma fine you for wasting my goddamn time!"

Nyambura: (laughing) " Boy, you better charge yourself - you've been wasting time since we met ."

( The whole room roars with laughter. Jaymo smirks, pulling Nyambura close for a quick kiss before the next case.)

Jaymo: " Alright, y’all - who’s next? Bring that drama !"

( The crowd erupts into cheers, and the drama lives on, hotter and wilder than ever.)
