Extended Scene 1 – “The Side-Chick Situation”

(Location: Inside the court. A jilted girlfriend, *Winnie*, drags her boyfriend *Timo*into Jaymo’s “court” after catching him with another woman.)

Jaymo: (grinning) "Aight, calm down. You bringin’ your relationship to *me* means it’s already fucked up. What’s the story?"

Winnie: (fuming) "This dumbass was out at Dunga Beach with a side-chick. *My* man eatin’ fish with another bitch?! Nah, that’s where I draw the line."

Timo: (shrugging) "First of all, Winnie—relax. It wasn’t even like that."

Winnie: (furious) "Like what, Timo? Like what?! You had your hands all over that bitch. Ain’t no way you accidentally end up at the beach with someone in a bikini!"

Jaymo: (laughing) "Man, I gotta hear this excuse. Timo, tell me: how do you ‘accidentally’ end up with another woman?"

Timo: (sweating) "I mean… we was just chillin’, vibin’. It wasn’t even serious. Just some casual… friendship shit, you know?"

Nyambura: (shaking her head) "Nigga, please. Friends don’t take their clothes off by the lake. Try harder."

(The crowd howls with laughter, and Winnie looks like she’s two seconds from exploding.)

Winnie: "Jaymo, I want *everything* from this clown—his hoodie, his PlayStation, even those fake-ass Yeezys. He playin’ me? Now I’m playin’ him!"

Jaymo: (to Timo) "Man, you better pay up before she repossesses your whole life."

Timo: "C’mon, Winnie, let’s talk about this!"

Winnie: (grinning) "Too late, motherfucker. I’m takin’ it all!"

Extended Romantic Drama – "Jaymo and Nyambura Clash"

(Location: Jaymo’s apartment. It’s late, and Jaymo and Nyambura are arguing after Nyambura finds out Jaymo’s been flirting with Shiro, the waitress.)

Nyambura: (pissed) "So you out here sweet-talking Shiro like I’m just some side piece? What the fuck, Jaymo?!"

Jaymo: (defensive) "It wasn’t even like that! Damn, Nyambura, you blowin’ shit way outta proportion."

Nyambura: (voice rising) "Nigga, proportion?! You were out there touchin’ her hand like you tryna propose! Don’t play dumb with me."

Jaymo: (frustrated) "I swear it wasn’t nothin'. Can’t a nigga even talk to a woman without catchin' a case?"

Nyambura: (furious) "You can *talk*, Jaymo. What you *can’t* do is make me feel like a clown. Fuck you and your little excuses!"

(Jaymo steps closer, voice low, trying to calm her down.)

Jaymo: "Look, Nyambura... I only got space for one crazy girl in my life—and it’s you."

(For a moment, they stare at each other, the tension thick enough to cut with a knife. Then, suddenly, Nyambura yanks Jaymo’s collar, pulling him into a rough kiss.)

Nyambura: (breathlessly) "You better keep it that way, nigga."
- - -

Fight Scene – "Jaymo and Pato Get Jumped"

(Location: A late-night alley. Jaymo and Pato run into some guys who are pissed about losing a case earlier.)

Thug #1: (smirking) "What’s good now, lawyer boy? You think talkin’ slick in court makes you tough on the street?"

Jaymo: (rolling his neck) "Man, I thought y’all were gonna move on. Guess some niggas just can’t take an L."

Pato: (nervous) "Jaymo, maybe we should—"

Jaymo: (cutting him off) "Fuck that. If they want smoke, let’s give 'em the whole pack."

(The thugs lunge. Fists fly. Jaymo dodges a punch and slams one thug into a wall. Meanwhile, Pato throws wild, sloppy punches—most of which miss.)

Pato: (panting) "Jaymo! I’m fightin’ for my *life* out here!"

Jaymo: (laughing) "Nigga, you fightin' like you underwater. Get it together!"

(Jaymo lands a clean uppercut on one thug, dropping him cold. Pato, somehow, manages to trip another guy with pure luck.)

Jaymo : "Damn, Pato. You fight like shit, but I gotta respect the hustle."

(The thugs scatter, leaving Jaymo and Pato catching their breath.)

Pato: (grinning) "A win’s a win, bro."

Big Twist – "Nyambura’s Betrayal"

(Location: Jaymo’s apartment. Jaymo discovers that Nyambura has been secretly working with Baba Reggie to scam him.)

Jaymo: (heartbroken) "You played me? After all that shit we been through?"

Nyambura: (coldly) "It’s not personal, Jaymo. Just business."

Jaymo: (angrily) "Fuck business! You said you were different—turns out you just like the rest of 'em."

Nyambura: (softly) "I told you not to fall for me, didn’t I?"

(Jaymo clenches his jaw, struggling to hold back the anger and betrayal.)

Jaymo: "You just made the biggest mistake of your life, Nyambura."

Nyambura: (smirking) "We’ll see, lawyer boy."

Jaymo: "You really think that knife scares me, old man? I’ve seen worse in the mirror."

Jaymo: (breathing hard) "Game over, Reggie. And guess what? I don’t need no court for this."

(He tosses Reggie’s phone into the lake and walks away as police sirens blare in the distance.)

Final Scene – "The Court Thrives"

(Location: Back at the bar-courtroom. Business is booming, and the cases are crazier than ever.)
